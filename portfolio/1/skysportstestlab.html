Sky Sports Test Lab

Create a high tech activation to support the latest Sky Tv promotions

As a part of the Sky Sports TV promotion I designed an experiential competition: the customer with the highest score will a Sky Package.

The competition was based on on trivia quiz and 2 skill games.

The quiz was on a large touchscreen and I used Surface Tablets registration and capturing scores.

For the registration I used fingerprint scanners. This way the customer can be recognized at each data entry point just with the touch of a finger

A real time leaderboard was displaying the highest scores.

At the end of each day every customer received a tailored email with the scores.

The promotion was happening simultaneously in several shopping centres across UK.

Real time intelligence data was provided to EM / Client to maximize the promotion
