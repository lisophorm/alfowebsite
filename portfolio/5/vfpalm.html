Palm-Vein Scanning, Vodafone VIP, Festivals 2012

Integrating an innovative hardware solution into everyday events

As part of Vodafone’s VIP program we took a truck containing 2,000 phone chargers to 8 festivals

We built a bespoke system that scanned consumers palms as a secure method for leaving their phone behind

The system told the staff exactly which locker to put the phone in according to its charger type

Upon collection of the phone the consumer scanned their hand and it told the staff exactly where to collect the phone from

Across all the festivals the system logged over 22,000 phones and not a single one was lost!
