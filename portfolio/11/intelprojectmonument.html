Intel Project Monument

Created a bespoke vending machine for Intel in Covent Garden, distributing over 400 retro gifts to members of the public

We were asked to create a viral video from a live event, that brought to life Intel’s campaign idea of Project Monument.

By creating a bespoke vending machine filled with nostalgic products, this event would be activated in an iconic location for one day.

The experience will capture the emotion and excitement of adults as they revisit their youth.

Bringing to life the excitement and nostalgia of Project Monument, we created a bespoke vending machine which distributed over 400 retro gifts in busy Covent Garden.

Inspiring participants to revisit their youth, consumers were immersed into the Project Monument message through participation in space hopper races, scalextrics races & a photo wall with retro fancy dress items.
