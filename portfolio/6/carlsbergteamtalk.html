England Team Talk Roadshow

Real time video generation to give the customers a unique personalized experience England Team Talk Roadshow

THE BRIEF Create an inspiring experiential campaign that encourages participation in Carlsberg’s “Probably the best team talk in the world…” competition and leverage Carlsberg’s sponsorship of the England football team in the run up to the 2010 World Cup.

THE EXPERIENCE Visitors enter the Carlsberg tour bus to record their own a team talk in the on-board video studio. Talks are then uploaded to the team talk youtube channel where they can be viewed by all. The process was completely automated, and the visitor could get their own video minutes after the recording. The road show also includes a speed kick and photo green screen.

THE RESULTS The Team Talk road show will visit 70 locations across 3 months, including: 13 Football clubs, including Wembley 50 Grocery Superstores 3 Trading HQ’s 3 Butlins resorts

The experiential campaign delivered 95% of the total BTL entries and produced an unprecedented sales up lift of 21%.
