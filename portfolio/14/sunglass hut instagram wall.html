Sunglass Hut - Instagram Wall

Taking an Instagram feed relating to the brand and displaying the photos in a dynamic way within the event space

The instagram wall has been used at two events for Sunglasses Hut and throughout the duration of the event the public were encouraged to post photos using instagram – by doing so they were entered into a competition.

When photos with the hashtag #styleitout were posted they would be collected and composed on the instagram wall on site. The screen would hold the most recent photos and make space by removing the oldest.

A moderation tool is also provided and there is the option of holding all posts or taking photos down should any be inappropriate

Throughout these events we had 310 photos uploaded to the wall via the #.

The instagram wall provides encouraging, digital and aesthetic value to the event space.
