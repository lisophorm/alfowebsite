Barclays Premier League Trophy Tour

Activating the largest football sponsorship in the world in nine global markets

The Barclays Premier League is the most watched league in the world; however, analysis of research showed that the majority of worldwide fans only have a brand relationship through their TV’s.

In order to deliver against the insight, we created the ultimate brand experience journey and toured it around the world.

The experience includes; an interactive museum, immersive projection sequence, intimate trophy moment and the ultimate memento, a photo of the visitor holding aloft the Premiership trophy (click here for case study).

The whole customer journey was tied together by state of the art fingerprint scanners.

The user could register at any interaction point or could just bring up their details swiping their finger.

To date the experience has engaged with over 46,000 loyal fans, with 96% of visitors rating the experience good to excellent (exit poll survey) and generating over 2m of estimated media value.
