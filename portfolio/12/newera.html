Creating an authentic MLB Baseball Card and uploading it straight to Facebook

As part of creating an ‘MLB Dugout’ in the New Era Store for 2 weeks, we built a photobooth app that took a picture of consumers and turned them into their own baseball card, users could then upload their personalised cards to Facebook.

The app allowed the user to select a position and a nickname that appeared on the overlay.

From just over 100 photos uploaded to Facebook, we generated 294 likes for MLB and 21,312 Facebook impressions.
