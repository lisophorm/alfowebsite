RFID and Facebook Connect – Vodafone Car Run, Budapest 2012

Use of RFID wristbands to maximise the amplification on social media of a live promotion.

WATCH THE VIDEO

- As part of an F1 fan zone in Budapest, I used RFID technology to amplify the on site activity by enabling visitors to automatically share their event experience with their friends on Facebook.

6 stations around the event had RFID enabled points where users could have a go on the assets and post their scores to their Facebook wall. The station that created more interest (over 30% of total interactions) was based on a green screen photo-mechanic: the consumer could sit into a real f1 car andsuper-imposed to a F1 track.

Users could register at any station or could swipe their RFID wristband to automatically bring up their details.

The system led to over 5,000 on-site consumer registrations which generated 300,000 Facebook unique impressions. The Facebook amplification continued post event for three days with other 46,000 impressions.
