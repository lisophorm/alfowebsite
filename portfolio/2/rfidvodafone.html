RFID and Facebook Connect – Vodafone Car Run, Budapest 2012

Use of RFID wristbands to maximise the amplification on social media of a live promotion.

As part of an F1 fan zone in Budapest, I used RFID technology to amplify the on site activity by enabling visitors to automatically share their event experience with their friends on Facebook.

6 stations around the event had RFID enabled points where users could have a go on the assets and post their scores to their Facebook wall.

Users could register at any station or could swipe their RFID wristband to automatically bring up their details

The system led to over 5,000 on-site consumer registrations which generated 300,000 Facebook impressions
