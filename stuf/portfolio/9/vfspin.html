MMS Mechanics - Vodafone Spin & Win

Supporting the Vodafone new store openings across UK MMS Mechanics - Vodafone Spin & Win

THE BRIEF: To modify three giant in-store BlackBerry handsets, incorporating a touch-screen interface to support the Vodafone New Store Opening activity across the UK.

THE EXPERIENCE: Vodafone customers were given the opportunity to send an MMS photo of their face to a short code – on arrival in-store, customer photos were already uploaded and displayed on the ‘roulette wheel’ onscreen. Customers could then spin the ‘roulette wheel’ and if it stopped on their photo they would instantly win a prize from the UEFA Champions League prize pool.

THE RESULT: Between November 2008 and February 2009, the campaign was launched in 18 stores over eight weekends and realised a 62% redemption rate from entrants to the game played instore.
