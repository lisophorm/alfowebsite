Greenscreen – The Barclays Premier League Trophy Tour

Your special moment lifting the Barclays Premier League Trophy in front of your teams home crowd

I created a touchscreen app that takes a photo of a consumer and superimposes them holding the Barclays Premier League Trophy and into a stadium of their choice.

The app travelled around the world to 10 different countries – each country required a different language and keyboard layout (including Arabic and Chinese layouts).

The app allowed users to complete the process without the constant monitoring of staff

Over 24,000 photos were taken across the 10 events

Over 11,000 users visited the microsite to download their phot

Users then could share the photo with friends on facebook and by email
