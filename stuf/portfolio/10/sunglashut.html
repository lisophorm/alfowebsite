Sunglass Hut ‘Style It Out’ Pop-Up Experience

Transforming a 26ft Airstream into a premium Sunglass Hut pop-up experience in Covent Garden, London

To showcase the latest Sunglass Hut collections, we transformed a 26ft Airstream into a premium Sunglass Hut pop-up experience in Covent Garden.

Our ‘Style It Out’ experience gave guests the opportunity to try on a full range of designer frames and have a free 15 minute style consultation with our Sunglasses Hut stylists, who gave style advice about the best frames to suit their looks, style and attitude.

Guests could showcase the different looks proposed by our stylists with our specially designed photo mechanic, allowing them to print out their photo as well as having it uploaded directly to their Facebook page.

Guests also had the chance to win a pair of return flights to Kuala Lumpur, courtesy of 5* Malaysia Airlines with our Instagram competition, where guests uploaded their images from our pop-up experience using the hashtag #styleitout.
