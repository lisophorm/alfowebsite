Barclays Premier League Trophy Tour

Creating a bespoke online solution to cater for international Pre and Post event communications Website

To go along side the Barclays Premier League Trophy Tour we created a website to support the pre and post campaign consumer interaction.

The website hosted a section for visitors to learn about the tour and its locations as well as reserve a time to visit the event.

After the event visitors could log-in using their event wristband and retrieve their greenscreen trophy photo – at this point we collected a range of data.

The website was built in relevant local languages including Chinese and Arabic

Over 24,000 visitors booked a timeslot for an event

More than 11,000 returned to the website to get their greenscreen photo

Overall the website was visited over 100,000 times
